School Life

Turner School

Was Established in 1953

Turner school is one of the oldest schools in Canberra and the building had not been complete until November that year.Turner school was not always called Turner school it was called Hartley Street Annexe. In the late 1960s the number of sudents decresed. In 2013 Turner school celebrated its 60th birthday.



School Library

Was Established in 2010

In Turner school there are book limits for all year groups so you don't take to many books home. If we go into the Library we have to have a Library bag. We only have 2 weeks with the book you borrow.
